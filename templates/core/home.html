Inside the Skill Grid Program

We don't just match profiles; we build skilled capabilities and protect lives on the ground.

🔍

1. Sourcing & Screening

We actively coordinate with rural communities to identify laborers impacted by agricultural drought or job losses. Each candidate is vetted with basic KYC and background checks.

Scope On-the-Ground
🎓

2. Professional Training

Supported by skilling veterans with 20+ years of training expertise. Candidates receive intensive practical training in technical crafts (carpentry, welding, mechanics) alongside crucial soft skills.

Curriculum Dual-layer Skills
🛡️

3. Welfare & Safety Auditing

No worker is sent to unsafe sites. We audit every client contractor to ensure minimum wage guarantees, standard work hours, health facilities, and verified housing.

Ethics 100% Compliant
How it works

Fast Matchmaking. Safe Transactions.

Our lightweight monolithic architecture connects employers with the NGO matching system instantly. Once a company posts a request, coordinators run SQL queries, filter by credentials/location, and deploy workers.

✓ Structured attendance and logging modules
✓ Seamless integration with local SQLite db for prototyping
