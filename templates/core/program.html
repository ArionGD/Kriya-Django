Our Legal & Welfare Compliance Charter

Project Kriya operates under a strict legal vetting charter designed by the SEA Movement. We believe that fair work goes beyond the payroll. Our audits secure the fundamental rights of all placed employees.

  • Mandatory adherence to State Minimum Wage Regulations with digital disbursals
  • Group medical safety insurance policies provided for all heavy labor placements
  • Bi-weekly site audits conducted by NGO coordinators to review meals & housing
  • Anonymous grievance hotline for placed workers to report legal or wage disputes
