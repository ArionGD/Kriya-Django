Grid Architecture

Coordination Made Transparent

Discover how the Kriya skilling database streamlines worker qualification, auditing, and secure placement tracking in real-time.

Employee Onboarding & skilling

Candidates register on the roster and enter structured vocational training modules. Only after completing the core course are they designated as "Ready for Placement" in the matching database.

Employer Vetting Audits

To safeguard employees, NGO coordinators audit registering employers for compliance on housing standards, clean meals, emergency medical aid, and fair daily minimum wages.

Real-Time Matching

Employers post job requirements specifying titles, wages, and site locations. Our platform automatically searches the certified database, matching workers who fit the criteria within local geographic bounds.

Active Deployment Tracking

Coordinators track check-ins, wages disbursed, and attendance logs through a unified NGO dashboard. This guarantees accountability, safety compliance, and direct conflict mediation.
