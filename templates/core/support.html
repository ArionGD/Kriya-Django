Employee / Candidate Guide

Candidates undergo comprehensive training before placement. The process follows a structured three-step verification path:

  • 1. Government ID Verification: Registration requires government-approved documentation and a registered mobile number.
  • 2. Core Certification: Candidate completes the 4-week practical vocational curriculum at the regional center.
  • 3. Placement Roster: Upon course assessment, candidate profiles are flagged as "Ready" in the matchmaking database.

Employer Compliance Audit

To safeguard employee rights, all workplace locations must pass a physical pre-audit checks checklist:

  • ✓ Minimum Wage: Daily salaries must meet state-regulated minimum base payouts via direct digital transfers.
  • ✓ Safety Gear (PPE): Hardhats, steel-toed boots, protective glasses, and harness vests must be supplied.
  • ✓ Lodging Standards: High-humidity proof rooms, fresh water access, and clean meal areas are monitored.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the training certification last?

Our vocational certification is valid for 24 months, after which candidates can attend a 1-day refresher safety workshop to extend validity.

What is the dispute mediation process?

When an employee logs a wage or environment dispute, NGO coordinators lock the assignment profile and conduct a site inspection within 24 hours.
