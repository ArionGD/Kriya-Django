User Guides & Documentation

Read guides on registering as an employee or auditing your business as an employer.

Employee / Candidate Guide

1. Create Your Profile

Sign up selecting the "Employee" role and add your phone number. This is where NGO coordinators will contact you.

2. Complete Training Courses

Your account will stay in "Training" status. Attend core skilling sessions held at the NGO center to receive placement certificates.

3. Active Placements

Once certified, the Kriya engine matches you to compliant sites. Log your daily attendance using the "Check-In" console buttons.

Employer / Recruiter Guide

1. Setup Organization Vetting

Register selecting the "Employer" role. NGO coordinators will schedule site visits to audit basic facilities, minimum wages, and housing.

2. Submit staffing Requirements

Use your console to post new requirements. Enter daily wages, locations, and safety details to trigger database searches.

3. Placement Audits

Track the dispatched employees deployed at your sites, confirm payroll dispersals, and report disputes instantly.
